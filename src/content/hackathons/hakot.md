## The hook

Empty trucks are costing our farmers money. We spent **24 hours** building a way to fix it.

## The weekend

Just finished my first-ever **overnight hackathon** at **Ship or Be Shipped**, hosted by **Build and Ship Philippines** at the **Kaya Founders** offices.

Together with **Edward**, **Eduardo**, and **Abby**, we built **Hakot**, an AI-assisted logistics marketplace for matching farmers with truck drivers and reducing empty backhauls.

## Results

Zero sleep and **three pivots** later, Hakot stood out among **80+ participants** and took **1st Runner-Up**.

Huge thanks to the mentors for the advice and to **Build and Ship Philippines** for organizing the event.

## The problem we were solving

Farmers and operators often run **empty backhauls** while produce still needs to move. Hakot makes those trips easier to see and match—fewer wasted miles, better farm-to-market flow.
